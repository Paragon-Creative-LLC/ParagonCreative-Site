Step 1 - In Person Meeting

We meet with you face to face or over the phone. We believe that over text or through messaging there is no way we could know exactly what you need.

Step 2 - Functionality

Once we have a strong idea of everything that you need to have your dream website, then we will start building all of the functionality you have requested into the site. Things like a virtual storefront, forms, or even slideshows.

Step 3 - Design and Refine

Once your site is able to do everything that you need, we then make sure it looks amazing doing it! Matching the site to your brand or if this is a rebrand, matching the site to your freshly designed new logo and color scheme.

Step 4 - In Person Overview

In the same way that we started this process, we want to end it knowing that you are confident in what we have provided you. We will walk you through everything you need to know on your site and provide proper documentation to go with it.

Final - Follow Up's

Even after your website has been completed, we are always available. Send us an email or fill out the Support form on our website and we will message or call you as soon as we see it!
